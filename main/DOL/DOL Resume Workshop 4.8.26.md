Purpose = present bg of candidate and highlight experience / skills

Application Tracking Software  - scans and scores vs how closely match qualifications and keywords relative. Scores accomplishments higher than exp.
Improper formatting can cause to fail the ATS systems.

After passing thru ATS - answer why should I hire you. Resumes often contain entire career regardless relevancy, nowadays focus on how applicant can meet company needs. You need to take a tailored approach.
Resumes should not be stagnant. Have to be flexible and targeted to specific factors to companies and specific positions.
You must ensure your resume connects the dots for the reader. Answer questions of what value do you bring. What specific skills do you have and how you help company reach its goals.
Question of why should you be hired. 

Marketing document: resume is a marketing document. Present your best self quickly. First 10/15 lines. Newspaper above the fold top half of printed newspaper can be seen at first glance, that's most important content to grab the reader attention and sell the newspaper. Same idea here. If submitting electronically, place emphasis on what can be seen on screen without scrolling further.

Font rules:
Arial, Calibri, Cambria recommended - readable modern and ATS friendly. Times New Roman is outdated. 
Name 14-20 point. Rest of doc 10-12 pt. 
When emphasizing text: bold = remember. Italics = forget (hide something ie italic dates if only a couple months or 15 yrs).
A little color is generally okay if it is readable. Stay away from yellows / pale colors. Consider audience. For some positions a pop can be okay but in another it might be negative.

Page count = specific to your situation. General rule is that 2 pages is okay if you have 10 or more years of exp and specific certs related to the job you apply for. If 2 pages, make sure it's at least 1 and 3/4 pages long. Avoid just a little section spilled to second page - use all real estate if using it. 
Should not be over 2 pages. Can use specific formatting to fit in 1 or 2 pages. If just over use margin size between 0.5" to 1". Layout tab in word -> margins -> normal to narrow or custom margins. Top Bottom 0.5, left right 0.7 for example.

Paragraph options: indent let 0 right 0 special none
spacing before 0 after 0 line spacing single.

Simple formatting is best. Some applicant tracking software is more finnicky than others. Make it past the robot. Include section headings. Summary, skills, exp, education, certs. Help ATS categorize accurately.

Bullets to showcase job duties, accomplishments, skills. 
Be consistent. Same margins left/right, section headings consistent, center or left. Ordering job title, employer, location city state.
Professional file name. "Firstname lastname Resume." Clearly shows that it's your resume. Save it in a doc or pdf format unless instructed otherwise. 

Nos on formatting resume: templates, graphics/pics, tables, headers/footers (like page numbers or etc don't show in ATS - no contact info here), charts, text boxes, acronyms w/o explanation (spell it out at least once).
DOL has ATS friendly templates: dol.ny.gov/resumes-cover-letters-and-job-applications
ATS cannot read text boxes.

Resume types. Choose the right format
Depends on circumstances. 
Functional, chronologicla, and skills based combination = 3 types. Also master resume and federal resumes.

Chronological resume:
Most traditional and widely recognized format. Features career progression in reverse chronological order - most recent job then backward. Individual with consistent and progressive work history. Targeting roles similar to prev positions and showcase career growth and stability.
Highlights career progression and highlights upwards rtajectory and increasing responjsibility, accomplishments, achievements in each role. Generally easy to read, familiar format for hiring managers. ATS friendly.

Functional Resume = changing careers, employment gaps, diverse or nonlinear experience. Functional resume features and benefits = highlight transferable skills from past experience to apply to any job regardless of role.
Functional resume also emphasizes lack of direct experience. Mask or hide gaps.
But not preferred by employers (bc hiding something) and requires strong summary or profile to be effective. Not easily read by ATS.

Combination resume both skills and exp. Diverse skillset and solid work history. And those who want to showcase specific skills while career progression. Flexibility in diff aspects of backgrounds.
Key benefits of combo resume highlights both skills and work exp. Offers flexibility in structuring resume. Can be easily tailored to specific job reqs.

Master resume = document all work. Save time and effort while applying for job, keep story consistent. Includes everything, detailed job desc, edu, skills (hard technical measurable from exp and edu, plus soft how to approach work and interact with people) etc. Also include projects, volunteer work, awards earned. When time to apply, review particular job desc, pull most relevant job desc from master resume = targeted and compelling app gets noticed.

Federal resumes = required to apply for federal jobs. Break many traditional resume writing rules. Reqs summer 2025. 

Non objective statements = summary statement. Don't use "I/me/my" personal pronouns. No subjective claims, verifiable facts. 
Avoid like hit the ground running or hard working bc they don't prove anything. 

Skills = exactly what you bring to the table. Personal highlight reel. Showcase what you can do, not just what you know. Keywords in skill section can help ATS and HR det if you're a good fit.

Tailor it to every job you apply for. Identify key skills the employer is looking for. The more specific the better. 
Conflict resolution, presentation writing. Soft skills = weave into bullets. Interpersonal like communications, teamwork, leadership. 1-2 hard skills / technical skills and sampling. 

Skills-based resume writing

Work history experience section is heart of resume. Consistent verb tense.
Caregiver in a break from health reasons = caregiving for a loved one for yourself, it's accurate.

Don't include anything about references on the resume.
