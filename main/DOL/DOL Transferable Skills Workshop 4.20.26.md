Skills important in any job
- Communication skills - actively listen, follow directions, verbal written or nonverbal (esp in interview)
- Interpersonal skills - people skills, are you connecting with people, are people understanding you, it's always okay to clarify, empathy and emotional intelligence, able to problem solve / conflict resolution
- Teamwork skills - how many times do you see teamwork in a job post, can you play nice in a sandbox, work together and get along with people, cooperate, engage
- Leadership skills - give a hand up to somebody if someone else needs it. Encourage and empower people. Be able to negotiate, discuss, compromise
- Technology/computer skills - AI is here, utilize it.

- Public speaking. Know audience. Not do a lecture hall; start small. Respect, engaging, don't talk down to anyone. Empowerment, learning. - Phone or other virtual skills

Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS)
- ATS often called Keyword Searching Technology
- ATS has become so prevalent that all job seekers need to understand how ATS impacts job search
- ATS is used by job search sites, employers, recruiters and others to shrink the pool of qualified candidates and prioritize and decide who to contact
- Once identify transferable skills and phrases and key words associated with skills, be able to utilize ATS to benefit
