Registered apprenticeships RA overview:
Always real job with on the job training under guidance of experienced worker. Skilled individuals often called journey workers. As more skills mastered, apprentice more valuable to sponsor. Often supplemented with classroom training, incl. relevant instruciton with the trade. Can include courses at trade school @ local community college, thru career/technical training program. Incl standards and training programs to ensure apprentice learning. Enter with all skills needed for a trade. Employers/sponsors conduct the programs.

Apprentices = full time employees who produce high quality work, earn an increasing scale of wages, dev skills under close supervision of accomplished skilled worker (ie journey worker). Work while mastering various skills over time, attend >= 144hrs of related instruction per year. Receive nationally recognized cert of completion.
Adv mfg, info tech, agriculture, healthcare.

Two main ways: 
1. recruitment to NYS registered apprenticeship program; meet all program eligibility reqs, like high school diploma, ability to lift 40 lbs, prior exp etc
2. Direct Entry program - helps individuals interested in RA gain skills needed to meet min eligibility reqs. DOL maintains directory of approved direct entry providers.

### 599 program
For unemployment insurance benes only.
Provision of UI: UI recipients enroll in full time approved training program - waive work search requirement.
Report enrollment in fulltime training. Not tuition asst program. 
Ask if receiving UI benefits, attending school or training program, career advisor regarding training opportunities.

https://dol.ny.gov/599-program

Need to be accepted into a program, be career specific, expected completion within 24 months, prove ability to ability to obtain regular LT employment in state/local job mkt and meet min or standard program. Meet with local career center and posess skills necessary.

Potential for extended benefits; if still attending 599 program when benefits exhausted, there may be maximum of 26 add'l weeks of benefits under UI Law S599.2, but if funding is exhausted, no funding. Add'l benefits not guaranteed.
DOL will est a waitlist dependent on factors - individuals have funds benefits. If anyone drops out, their allotted funds for program would be available. 599 program doesn't provide tuition asst. Training provider/uni fin aid to discuss.
Grades / progress maintenance. Must maintain passing grades to be eligible, waived from work search req. Participants periodically submit copy of grades, schedules or other proof from training that enrollment consistents min req of 12 credit hours and satisfactory progress in approved training.

Can also be used to earn high school equivalency as long as 12 classroom hrs a week, definite training completion date, 24 months of approval date.

Application for training approval submit within first 13 week of claiming UI to receiving maximum benefits. Submit application immediately upon acceptance into school/training.

### Federal Bonding Program FBP
Hiring incentive for job seekers facing challenges for employment - significant reduce risk of hiring. 6 months of no cost fidelity bonding coverage to biz - federal bonding program promoted to biz and job seekers, easy for biz to apply for and obtain benefits. 99% success rate, 10k+ individuals back in this program.

dol.ny.gov/federal-bonding-program-0

fidelity bonds serve job seekers with employment barriers - insurance for biz protects loss of money from dishonesty - theft, embezzlement, forgery, etc. Increasing likelihood of job seeker with barriers. 
Serves as enticement for companies and employers to hire job applicants, ie people with involvement in criminal justice system, poor credit/declared bankruptcy, little to no work history, receiving public asst benefits (subsidies, etc), state or local youth employment program participation, completed alcohol/substance treatment. Also emplyoemtn to prevent a layoff - involved in criminal system during employment, fidelity bonding may be needed to prevent employee from being laid off. Transfer/promo eligilibty makes access to money/property/info making biz uneasy. 
Bond lasts 6 mo and can purch add'l 6 mo of continued coverage.

Restrictions: meet min legal age for working in NYS, and be paid wages with federal taxes auto deducted. Work any full/part time job, incl LT temp and temp-to-perma positions.

Either employer or applicant may ask a bond be issued. Request to local bonding coordinator. Total of $5k bond coverage usually issued with no deductible amount of liability. Bonds can also be issued for 10/15/20/25. Must have justification and approvla by state bonding coordinator. Typically only requires minimal explanation.

Once date is set for applicant to start work, bond can be signed instantly. Bond is self terminating. Must contact local bonding coordinator @ local career center.

### Work Opportunity Tax Credit WOTC
Federal tax credit available to biz to hire individual in target groups facing barriers to employment. 1200-9600 to hire qualified individuals. Average credit amount 2400. 
dol.ny.gov/wotc-program
Individuals must complete 120 - 400 hours of work for 1500-2400 credit; apply within 28 days of first day of work.
Apply for and receive a certification verifying new employees' position within target group before claiming tax credit. Claim WOTC against income or payroll taxes.

WOTC eligible target groups:
- Veterans
- Ex felons
- Summer Youth
- LT Unemployed invidiuals
- Vocational Rehab Referral
- Designated community residents (clinton/montgomery countires/syracuse/lower manhattan/south bronx/yonkers)
- Recipients of ST Temp Financing, SNAP, SSI
WOTC section on program target groups and amount of money per group.

Advocate yourself to potential employers if you qualify for WOTC program. Can found on NY DOL website.

### Self Employment Asst Program SEAP
SEAP allows out of work individuals to work full time on starting own biz while claiming UI benefits.
Program participants waived from work search requirement. Work on startup fulltime.
Opporutnity to build networks/support thru training and counselling. 2 required face to face meeting with counselor and 20 hrs of entrepreneurial training.
Profits earned are for the biz to keep, do not affect UI benefits.
Written acceptance to SEAP required.

SEAP: 18 yrs of age + >= 13 eweks of UI benefits. Receive invitation letter from NYS DOL or categorized as discloated worker. New SEAP applicant means you cannot apply to it in the past. Cannot have previously approved. SEAP orientation must be completed on acceptance.

SEAP eligibility for biz proposal:
1. Proposed business must be in NYS
2. Applicant must be willing to work full time to launch their biz
3. Applicant must be an active owner
4. Applicant must be first time biz owner of proposed biz type (not similar in nature to one you already own or have operated in the past)
5. Applicant must be prepared with a clear biz idea/plan before applying
